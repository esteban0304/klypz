INV inventory & crafting
Progression: punch logs → planks → sticks → crafting table (2×2 planks) → right-click it for 3×3 → wooden pickaxe (2×) → stone (4×) → iron (6×) → deep down: gold (12×, fragile), diamond (8×, lasts forever), redstone & mountain emeralds. Wander far to find villages, abandoned mineshafts and hidden dungeons — the world never ends. Tools wear out — watch the durability bar!
